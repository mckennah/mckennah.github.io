Spotify API

Type in an artist's name and their albums that are available on Spotify will appear.
Searches for artists without albulms on Spotify (cough, Taylor Swift) will bring up albums related to that search term.
Click on an album an a short audio clip will play. Spelling does need to be correct.
Also, not every album has an audio clip, (thanks Spotify) so try a couple!
