Martingale Collars

All of our collars are made with high-quality, durable fabric. They are also hypoallergenic!
The martingale collar is recommended for greyhounds; the extra loop allows the collar to tighten when pulled. Because of their large neck and small head size, greyhounds can slip out of traditional collars.
